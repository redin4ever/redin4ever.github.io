About Our Kickstarter Clone

This is a simplified, front-end only demonstration of a Kickstarter-like platform, built with Vue.js and Tailwind CSS. It showcases basic project listing, detail viewing, and navigation.

Our Mission

To empower creators to bring their innovative ideas to life by connecting them with a passionate community of backers. We believe in the power of collective support to foster creativity and innovation.

Technologies Used

  • Vue.js 3: For reactive components and state management.
  • HTML5: For the semantic structure of the web page.
  • Tailwind CSS: For utility-first, responsive styling.
  • JavaScript: For core logic and interactivity.
